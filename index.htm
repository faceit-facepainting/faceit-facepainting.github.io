About Me

My name is Sarah Crites. I am 20 years old and a Sophomore at Loyola University Chicago. I was trained as a Face Painter in 2013 at Six Flags Great America. Since I moved to Chicago for school I have started face painting at kids' parties, youth events, and school carnivals to help pay for school and because I absolutely love it.

Pricing

Pricing is negotiable and depends on factors such as:

  • Availability
  • Distance
  • Ease of Travel
  • Size of Event
  • Age of Guests
  • Special Requests

The base rate before special accommodations is $20/hour.
